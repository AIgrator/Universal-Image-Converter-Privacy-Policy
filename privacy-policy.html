1. Introduction

Welcome to Universal Image Converter ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Universal Image Converter mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We DO NOT collect any personal information from you.
  • We do not require registration
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do not collect payment information (handled by Google Play)

2.2 Images and Files

All image processing happens locally on your device.
  • Images you select for conversion are never uploaded to any server
  • All processing is done offline using the ImageMagick library on your device
  • Converted files are saved to your device's storage (Download/Image Converter folder)
  • We do not access, store, or transmit your images

2.3 Device Information

We do not collect device information.

The App does not:

  • Collect device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts or call logs
  • Monitor your browsing history

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to use.
